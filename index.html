Managed TikTok Account Analytics

Authorized TikTok profile and post display for managed creator and business accounts.

Genie Market is a web application for managed TikTok creator and business accounts. Users connect through TikTok Login, authorize access in Sandbox, and Genie Market displays profile and recent post data in the website dashboard.

Authorized Access

Users connect TikTok accounts through approved TikTok Login flows and grant access only to the scopes required for profile and post display.

Profile and Post Display

Genie Market displays connected account information and recent posts in a website dashboard for review, reporting, and managed account visibility.

Reporting Workflow

The displayed account data supports structured reporting and analytics workflows for managed TikTok creator and business accounts.

How Genie Market Works

1. Connect Account

User grants permission through TikTok Login.

2. Retrieve Profile

Basic account information is used to identify the connected account.

3. Retrieve Posts

The app retrieves the account's video list for website display.

4. Show Dashboard

The website displays managed account profile and recent posts.

Scopes Used:
• user.info.basic — used to display the connected TikTok account profile information.
• video.list — used to display the connected TikTok account’s recent videos on the website dashboard.
