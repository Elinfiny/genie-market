Managed TikTok Account Analytics

Authorized TikTok profile and post display for managed creator and business accounts.

Genie Market is a web application for managed TikTok creator and business accounts. After a user grants permission through TikTok Login, the app retrieves basic profile information and video lists to display account content on the website and support reporting and analytics workflows.

Authorized Access

Users connect TikTok accounts through approved TikTok Login flows and grant access only to the scopes required for profile and post display.

Profile and Post Display

Genie Market displays connected account information and recent posts in a website dashboard for reporting, review, and managed account visibility.

Reporting Workflow

The displayed account data supports structured reporting and analytics workflows for managed TikTok creator and business accounts.

How Genie Market Works

The application is designed for managed TikTok creator and business accounts. A user authorizes access through TikTok Login, Genie Market retrieves permitted profile and video list data, and the website displays the connected account’s content in a dashboard.

1. Connect Account User grants permission through TikTok Login.
2. Retrieve Profile Basic account information is used to identify the connected account.
3. Retrieve Posts The app retrieves the account’s video list for website display.
4. Show Dashboard The website displays managed account profile and recent posts.

Scopes Used

  • user.info.basic — used to display the connected TikTok account profile information.
  • video.list — used to display the connected TikTok account’s recent videos on the website dashboard.
