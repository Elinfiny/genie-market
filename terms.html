Terms of Service

Genie Market is a website application for managed TikTok creator and business account review and dashboard display.

Use of the Service

Users may connect a TikTok account through TikTok Login and authorize approved access in order to display profile and recent post information on the Genie Market website dashboard.

Authorized Scopes

  • user.info.basic — connected account profile display
  • video.list — recent post display in the dashboard

Restrictions

Genie Market only uses approved TikTok account access for the dashboard functions described on the website. Unauthorized or unrelated access is not requested.
