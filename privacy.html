Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Genie Market respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled in connection with the Genie Market app and related analytics workflows.

1. Information We Collect

Genie Market may collect and process social media analytics data, content performance metrics, and account-related data that the user has explicitly authorized for access.

2. How We Use Information

Data is used only for internal analytics, content performance tracking, product validation, and operational decision-making.

3. Data Sharing

Genie Market does not sell personal data. Data is not shared with third parties except when required for technical integrations used by the user, such as analytics, automation, or database tools.

4. Data Storage

Authorized data may be stored in connected tools and systems used by Genie Market for analytics and reporting purposes.

5. User Control

Users may revoke access to connected services at any time through the relevant platform settings.

6. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect authorized data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: contact@geniemarket.app
