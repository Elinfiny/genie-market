Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Genie Market collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users access the Genie Market website or use connected service features.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Basic contact information provided through direct communication.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device information, and access logs.
  • Service usage information related to website interactions.
  • Authorized third-party platform data provided after user consent.

2. TikTok-Related Data

If a user authorizes Genie Market through TikTok Login or another approved TikTok integration flow, Genie Market may access limited data permitted by the scopes approved for the application and granted by the user.

Such data may include:

  • Basic account information.
  • Limited content metadata, such as a list of videos accessible through the approved scope.

Genie Market does not access TikTok data without authorization and does not request TikTok credentials directly from users.

3. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the service.
  • Support internal analytics, workflow automation, and reporting.
  • Review content performance and product validation workflows.
  • Respond to support, compliance, or security requests.
  • Enforce legal, contractual, or operational requirements.

4. Data Sharing

Genie Market does not sell personal data. We may share information only when reasonably necessary:

  • With infrastructure or service providers supporting website or workflow operations.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid governmental request.
  • To protect security, rights, property, users, or the integrity of the service.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain security, support legitimate business operations, comply with law, or resolve disputes.

6. Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. User Rights

Depending on applicable law, users may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain personal information. Users may also request information about how their data is used.

8. Third-Party Services

The website or service may rely on third-party tools, platforms, or infrastructure providers. Those services may process information under their own terms and privacy policies.

9. Children

Genie Market is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to provide valid consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective or last updated date.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact: contact.geniemarket@gmail.com
