Privacy Policy

Genie Market is a website used for managed TikTok creator and business account review, profile display, and recent post display.

When a user connects a TikTok account through TikTok Login, Genie Market may receive authorized account data that is limited to the approved scopes requested during authorization.

Data Used

  • user.info.basic — used to display the connected TikTok account profile information.
  • video.list — used to display recent posts from the connected TikTok account in the website dashboard.

Data Handling

Genie Market uses authorized account information only for website dashboard display, review demonstration, and reporting workflow visibility. Genie Market does not request unrelated account permissions.
